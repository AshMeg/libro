A letter to a friend


Let us get started with the end, and maybe then the beginning will finally make sense. A message was received outlining - as one would expect the doctor would tell you when the dreaded big C finally made its way through your body - of pending nuptials. Clinically hardly gives the word justice, but English - as it often does - falls short of conveying the lack of empathy hidden between those black letters and white spaces. The message stated that my mother had given her diary acceptance through Facebook, commiting herself to cross-world travel mission to see someone throw too much money at a photoshoot so everyone else can smile and pretend they are still friends.

But we get ahead of ourselves. This message came from someone that had been known for the good part of 25 years. Someone who had received friendship in abundance, while giving little of herself up in return. A clinical message of such bleached emotion stating towards the fact that she had now gotten 153 likes on Facebook for a top pinterest inspired ring on her left hand, as red blotched eyes, with a full face of makeup and hair windswept from the water that graces the back porch of the house, was captured in a moment from someone who conveniently was there at the time.

Bitter, I hear you say? No, friends, there are no sour lemons here. Merely someone overly fascinated with true colours - something that Pocahontas can attest to, as they travel on the polluted wind and leave a foul smell behind them. This is more of an experiment looking into the psychology behind how someone can not understand what an absolute asshole they have been, and missed a prime opportunity to build any bridges - whether of the swing variety or that of the Golden instead - based on one momentous occasion. Instead there was a surgical precision to the very bold ‘Fuck you’ that was squashed in every space between the full stops and and lack of emojis.

Even the all-powerful Pointing has no control about the blind texts it is an almost unorthographic life One day however a small line of blind text by the name of Lorem Ipsum decided to leave for the far World of Grammar.
